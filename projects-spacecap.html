SpaceCap: a 3-DOF robotic arm to capture small debris in space

Space debris is a growing threat to operational satellites and future missions. SpaceCap is a 3-DOF robotic arm designed to demonstrate controlled debris capture via teleoperation at lab scale, validating the core mechanics of reach, align, and grasp before scaling to an on-orbit system

This early-stage prototype operates within a cylindrical workspace and reaches target coordinate with precision and consistency. It can grasp representative object with widths below 50mm and masses under 20g.
