SpaceCap: a 3-DOF robotic arm to capture small debris in space

SpaceCap is a 3-DOF robotic arm designed to capture small debris objects via teleoperation, simulating on-orbit grasping in a lab environment. The arm follows an RPP (rotational-prismatic-prismatic) config with a 2-jaw end-effector gripper, actuated via stepper motors and servos controlled through an Arduino interface.

This early-stage prototype operates within a cylindrical workspace and reaches target coordinate with precision and consistency. It can grasp representative object with widths below 50mm and masses under 20g.
